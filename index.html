Tina's Grill

Website Redesign

February 2025

"A family owned restaurant on the south west side of Aurora
  where everyone knows your name!"

- Tina's Grill, on Facebook, 2019

The original website for Tina's Grill had important issues regarding the website’s older style and monotonous appearance, missing information, and its inefficient layout. The redesign addresses each of these issues. Visually, by adding bold graphics, new colour shades, and unique fonts to enhance readability and strengthen the restaurant’s fun tone. Content-wise by removing repeated information and adding much needed missing information. As for usability, the project is designed with the user in mind, adding shortcuts for easy use and information hierarchy for readability. The result is a fuller, more pleasant and efficient experience navigating Tina's Grill website.
